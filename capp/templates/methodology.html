1. Car

A car can pollute through various mechanisms depending on its propulsion method, whether it's petrol, diesel, or electric power. In Norway, thanks to extensive vehicle electrification, electric cars have indirect emissions as low as 40 g CO2/km (related to manufacturing and electricity generation). In 2025, Norway aims 100% of new private cars to be zero-emission.
